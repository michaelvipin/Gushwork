Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing
Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion

The raw material is melted and formed into a continuous pipe profile under controlled temperatures and pressure.

  • Advanced die-head design

Advanced Vacuum Cooling

The extruded pipe passes through high-efficiency vacuum and water spray cooling tanks to solidify the molten material rapidly.

  • Multi-stage heat reduction
  • Eliminates internal stress

Accurate Sizing & Calibration

Specialized calibration sleeves and automated ultrasonic thickness sensors guarantee the pipe's exact outer diameter and internal tolerance.

  • Micro-millimeter precision
  • 360-degree laser scanning

Rigorous Quality Control

Continuous inline testing ensures complete structural integrity, checking for burst strength, impact resistance, and elongation.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • Automated flaw detection

Automated Precision Marking

High-resolution inkjet and laser printers embed permanent manufacturing data, pressure classes, and standards directly onto the pipe surfaces.

  • High visibility coding
  • Abrasion-resistant prints

Planetary Cutting

Advanced synchronized planetary cutters produce perfectly flat, burr-free chamfered edges automatically without halting the continuous extrusion.

  • Swarf-free cutting
  • Automated length measurement

Coiling & Packaging

Pipes are sequentially bundled or loaded onto fully automated twin-station coilers for safe, compact, and ready-to-ship arrangements.

  • Tension-controlled coiling
  • UV-protected strap bundling
